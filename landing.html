VARIETIES

Amber

This red, medium-sized fruit becomes fully ripe in mid-October. The sweet apples are native to Kashmir in Northern India and are the country's only indigenous apple.

American Trel

A small and round shaped and quite juicy.It is very crispy and tasty. The skin of the fruit is white greenish and sweet.

Red Delicious

Red delicious is a medium sized apple,with a tall conical shape. A very popular and widely cultivated variety of apple that ripens in mid-September. Its flesh is greenish white, grainy and juicy.

Maharaji

A large-sized apple with bright red color on a green base with conspicious dots. Flesh is crisp,very juicy,acidic and aromatic.

Hazratbal

A quickly perishable variety that ripens in early July. It is the oldest variety of apples cultivated in the valley and is mostly consumed domestically

Golden Delicious

Golden delicious apples are an American variety belonging to the Rosaceae family. The apples were discoverd growing as a chance seedling in west Virginia in the early 20th century.
